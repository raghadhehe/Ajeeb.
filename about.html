Our Story

Bringing joy to Bahraini children for over 45 years.

Bahrain's Favourite Toy Store Since 1979

Ajeeb Store was founded in 1979 with a simple mission: bring the world's best toys to the children of Bahrain. What started as a small shop in Manama has grown into the Kingdom's most trusted toy destination, stocking over 1,500 products from leading global brands.

The name "Ajeeb" meaning "wonderful" or "amazing" in Arabic captures exactly what we believe every toy should make a child feel.

Today, Ajeeb Store serves thousands of Bahraini families through our retail stores and our fast-growing online shop. We remain proudly family-owned and deeply committed to the community we grew up in.

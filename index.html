Why Every Child Deserves Ajeeb

A little about what makes us different.

At Ajeeb Store, we believe that the right toy at the right age can spark a lifelong passion. That's why every product on our shelves is hand-picked by our team of child development enthusiasts who understand the power of play. From LEGO sets that teach spatial reasoning to arts and crafts kits that build fine motor skills, each toy in our collection has been chosen with purpose.

We've been serving Bahraini families since 1979 over four decades of birthdays, Eids, and everyday surprises. Generations of children have grown up with Ajeeb toys in their hands, and many of those children now bring their own kids to shop with us. That cycle of trust and joy is the most meaningful thing we've built.

Our Manama store stocks over 1,500 unique products, and our online shop delivers across Bahrain within 1–3 business days. Whether you're after the latest LEGO City set, a classic Barbie Dreamhouse, or a safe sensory toy for a newborn, Ajeeb has you covered. Shop with confidence every item is CE-certified, age-appropriate, and backed by our 14-day return guarantee.
